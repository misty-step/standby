Make Standby hear any Mac call, with Teams as the first dogfood meeting.

Chosen design: local microphone plus system/app audio capture first, real transcription behind a source adapter, deterministic proposal approval, and an out-of-request local CLI worker for read-only research. Vexa, Recall, Teams, Zoom, and Meet integrations become optional providers, not the product core.

CHOSEN DESIGN

LocalMacAudioSource is the primary capture path.

First worker is read-only research with negative sandbox proof.

verify-local-capture-smoke.sh, verify-real-transcriber-smoke.sh, verify-local-transcript-fixture.sh, verify-worker-runner.sh, verify-worker-sandbox.sh, verify-ui-states.sh, and gated verify-live-teams-local.sh.

Stop if capture lacks macOS permission, audio lanes stay silent, transcription is stubbed, or worker auth blocks.

STANDARDS

Real capture, real transcription, real worker, durable events, honest failure states, and no transcript-to-mutation path.

ACCEPTANCE

Teams audio routed through the Mac creates transcript evidence; an approved card launches a local CLI and returns an artifact or failure receipt.

VERIFY

Capture smoke, generated-audio transcriber smoke, transcript fixture replay, worker sandbox, UI state walk, live Teams local capture, and existing repo gate.

COMMUNICATE

Report exact readiness: fixture-ready, capture-ready, transcript-ready, worker-ready, live Teams-ready, or dogfood-ready.

STOP IF

Permissions fail, no audio arrives, transcription is not real, worker sandbox fails, approval hangs, or UI cannot show failure as first-class state.

CURRENT STATE

Demo route seeds transcript spans; approval calls MockResearchWorker; UI auto-starts demo and labels it live.

CHANGE SHAPE

Add TranscriptSource, a local capture/transcriber boundary, and WorkerRunner. Keep app/provider details behind adapters.

WHY THIS WINS

It proves the any-call product promise immediately. Teams-first becomes a dogfood test of the universal path, not a bespoke integration.

SCOPE

Goal: local audio capture from a real Teams call and real approved read-only research output.

Non-goals: Teams media bot first, Vexa-first dependency, Graph post-meeting import, repo mutation, Slack/email sends, perfect diarization.

Invariant: transcript text is quoted evidence, never executable instruction.
